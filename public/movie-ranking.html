Sarah's Favorite Movies of ALL TIME

The best of the best

Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers

This is the best of the Lord of the Rings and subsequent Hobbit trilogies. It has a mixture of action, humor, and heart.

Pan's Laberynth

I don't normally like theis kind of movie, but it was just so beautifully done with the visuals and the soundtrack that stays with you.

The Princess Diaries

This is what dreams are made of. Such a fun, feel-good movie.
